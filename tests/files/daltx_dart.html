DART Board Meetings Information

The DART Board of Directors meetings convene at 6:00 p.m. on the fourth Tuesday of each month, unless specified differently on the calendar. Standing Committee meetings are generally held on the second Tuesday of each month. *July, November, and December have one meeting date only. Meeting dates are subject to change.

 

 

Live Stream and Board Video Archives

If you are unable to attend a DART Board meeting in person, all meetings are streamed live and recorded for on-demand viewing.

speaker_notesPublic Comments

DART is committed to openness and transparency. Public comments are accepted at the Audit, Committee of the Whole, and Board of Directors meetings. Anyone wishing to address the DART Board of Directors can do so in-person at the DART Headquarters at 1401 Pacific Avenue during the public comment sections of the Audit, Committee of the Whole, and/or the Board of Directors' meetings.

Please complete a “REQUEST TO ADDRESS THE BOARD” public comment card prior to speaking to the public comments section of the agenda for Audit, Committee of the Whole, and/or Board of Directors' meetings. Anyone wishing to address the Audit Committee or Committee of the Whole must speak on an item listed on the agenda.

Please note that DART will not accept public comments submitted by email.


For 2025, the public can address the full Board on the dates listed in the calendar above.

The Paratransit Accessibility Advisory Group (PAAG) is to help DART in determining the needs of the disability community and to broaden DART’s community outreach to persons with disabilities.
